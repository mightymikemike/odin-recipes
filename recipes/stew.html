Casserole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Beef
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 2 Cups of Beef Stock
  • 4 Cups of Cubed Potatoes
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • Seasoning of your choice

    • Directions

    1. Gather ingredients
    2. Cook Beef until it reaches the proper temperature
    3. Chop Potatoes and Veggies
    4. Add water and stock and bring to a boil
    5. Add all ingredients to pot and season accordingly
