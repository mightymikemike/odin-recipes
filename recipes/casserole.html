Casserole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups of instant white rice
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can of cream of celery soup
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of butter

    • Directions

    1. Gather ingredients
    2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
    3. Stir chicken, water, rice, and soups in dish; season with salt and pepper
    4. Arrange butter evenly over top of chicken mixture
    5. Bake for about 1 hour
